hardening the viewport render loop against MuJoCo Renderer `__del__`

launch. The freeze is caused by (a) MuJoCo's Renderer `__del__` raising
`AttributeError('_gl_context')` during interpreter shutdown, which crashes
the viewport cleanup, and (b) the render loop's QTimer continuing to fire
after the window closes, blocking clean shutdown. These are blocking issues.

clean stop(); updated `main_window.py` closeEvent to stop viewport first;
launch smoke test in `test_viewport.py`.
